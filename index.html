Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Tap2Rec

Tap2Rec respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

Data Collection

Tap2Rec does not collect, store, or share personal data.

Audio Recording

The app records audio only when the user explicitly starts a recording. All audio files are stored locally on the user's device.

No Cloud Storage

Tap2Rec does not upload audio recordings or any data to external servers.

Permissions

The app requires access to the device microphone solely to record audio. No other sensitive permissions are used.

Data Sharing

Tap2Rec does not share any user data with third parties.
