Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Tap2Rec handles information when you use the application. Tap2Rec is developed and published by Wallaby Tech Labs.

2. Data Collection

Tap2Rec does not collect, store, process, sell, or share personal data from users.

3. Audio Recordings

Tap2Rec records audio only when the user explicitly starts a recording. The app does not record audio without user action.

All audio recordings are stored locally on the user's device. Tap2Rec does not access, upload, transmit, or store audio recordings on external servers.

4. Permissions

Tap2Rec requires access to the device microphone solely to allow the user to record audio. This permission is essential for the core functionality of the app.

5. Local Storage

Recordings created with Tap2Rec are saved locally on the user's device. Users are responsible for managing, deleting, or sharing their own recordings.

6. Third-Party Services

Tap2Rec does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking tools, or cloud storage services in this version of the app.

7. Data Sharing

Tap2Rec does not share user data with third parties.

8. User Control

Users can delete their recordings directly from their device. Since Tap2Rec does not collect or store user data externally, there is no external account or server-side data to delete.

9. Children's Privacy

Tap2Rec is not specifically designed for children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.
